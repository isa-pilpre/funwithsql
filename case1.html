Case 1: Holiday Activities

Scenario: Four friends are debating what fun activities they can do during the Holiday Season. They talk about "going skiing," "reading comic books," "binging on Christmas movies," "staying home with family," or "catching up on homework". Each friend gives their opinion on each activity, choosing from STRONGLY DISAGREE, DISAGREE, NEUTRAL, AGREE, STRONGLY AGREE.

Question: Please show the name of each friend, the number of friends who agree that "Staying home with family" is a fun Holiday activity, and their level of agreement (Strongly Agree or Agree).

Answer Format: Please display:

  • name
  • number_of_friends_who_agree
  • agreement_level

Table: holiday

id name activity opinion
1AmayaSkiingSTRONGLY AGREE
2IbanSkiingNEUTRAL
3MaxSkiingSTRONGLY AGREE
4SoleneSkiingDISAGREE
13AmayaStaying home with familyAGREE
14IbanStaying home with familyAGREE
15MaxStaying home with familySTRONGLY AGREE
16SoleneStaying home with familyAGREE

Solution:

SELECT 
    name,
    COUNT(*) AS number_of_friends_who_agree,
    opinion AS agreement_level
FROM holiday
WHERE activity = "Staying home with family"
      AND opinion IN ("AGREE", "STRONGLY AGREE")
GROUP BY name, opinion;

Expected Output:

name number_of_friends_who_agree agreement_level
Amaya1AGREE
Iban1AGREE
Max1STRONGLY AGREE
Solene1AGREE
